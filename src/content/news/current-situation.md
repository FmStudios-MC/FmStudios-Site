Hello. As you know, I'm currently working on “Create Unbound.” (More or less.) In fact, not much has happened on the development front. There are several reasons for this.

The first reason is that I don't have much free time because of my training as an IT specialist. If things go well, I might have 3 or 4 hours after work to spend on the computer. Most of the time, I just watch videos or TV shows because I don't feel like doing anything else. Even when I do feel like doing something, I just want to play video games.

The second reason is that I simply don't have the motivation right now to play Minecraft or work on it. I know I've already postponed “Create Unbound” so many times and have been neglecting the community. In the last few months alone, we've lost over 40 members from the Discord server.

The third reason is that I’m not really a very good developer. I get the feeling you’re expecting something amazing for “Create Unbound,” but it’ll probably end up being just like any other modpack. I work with AI a lot myself, but I don’t think it can help me in any way. I’m also grateful for the help some people have offered me, but I simply can’t accept it.


I'll be working on “Create Unbound,” but don't expect anything mind-blowing. There will be a community server again. However, the modpack's features won't be anything special. Quests probably won't be included either, or if they are, they'll be half-hearted.

I also plan to work on other projects besides Minecraft—useful programs and other stuff. I hope you can understand, and if not, I’m sorry. There’s no release date for “Create Ubound” yet, but I’m aiming for the third quarter of 2026.
